TEAM LEADERS

DUERDEN & KEANE'S team leaders manage and deliver a wide range of scientific and technical services drawing on the engineering, biological, chemical and management skills of its professional team members.

  • Both Dr. Duerden and Ms. Keane are experienced environmental professionals with significant expertise in the area of Environmental Management Systems. They are skilled and accomplished trainers and practitioners in the field of environmental management, with over 50 years of collective experience, both as regulators (with Environment Canada) and as private consultants.
  • Dr. Duerden and Ms. Keane have assisted private sector and public agencies with developing and auditing environmental management systems, and are authors of a practical resource guide for designing and implementing EMSs in the public sector.
  • They are both Environmental Professionals - Compliance Environmental Auditors (EP-CEA)and Environmental Professionals EMS Lead Auditors (EP-EMS(LA)), certified by the Canadian Environmental Certifications Approvals Board (CECAB).
